General Public

Regarding the general public, residents of a city can use the visualizations to gain insight and gain perspective on how they are contributing to carbon emissions. Especially when it comes to personal decisions that an individual can make in a day, the statistics and data provided can help people make changes that are more enviornment friendly. Also, this can provide a general awareness of how a city's emission trends are doing and potentially spark change on a larger scale among communities.
